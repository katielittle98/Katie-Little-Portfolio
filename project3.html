Project Overview

TinDog is a 'dating' app specifically designed for our furry friends. With TinDog, your pet can meet up with other dogs for a play date. This innovative platform connects dogs and their owners, allowing them to create profiles and search for compatible matches based on breed, size, personality, and more. TinDog provides a safe and enjoyable space to meet other dogs in your area.
